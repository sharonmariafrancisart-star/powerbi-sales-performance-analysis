 📊 Sales Performance Analysis (2015–2018)

🔹 Project Overview
This project analyzes four years of sales data (2015–2018) to identify revenue trends, growth patterns, and key business drivers across segments, regions, and product categories.

---

🔹 Business Questions
- How has overall sales trended over time?
- Which customer segment drives the most revenue?
- Which region contributes the highest sales?
- What is the Year-over-Year (YoY) growth rate?
- Which product category performs best?

---

🔹 Key Insights
- Sales grew consistently from 2015 to 2018 despite seasonal fluctuations.
- The Consumer segment contributes over 50% of total revenue.
- The West region generates the highest regional sales.
- Technology leads all product categories in total sales contribution.

---

🔹 Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX (Time Intelligence functions)  
- Data Modeling  
- Power Query  

---

🔹 DAX Measures
- Total Sales  
- Total Orders  
- Average Order Value  
- Sales Last Year  
- YoY Growth %
